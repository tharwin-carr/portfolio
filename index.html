Dog Quiz App

This app tests your knowledge about "man's best friend". It keeps track of right and wrong answers then presents the final score at the end. This project was an assignment for BLOC during our jQuery module. We were able to choose any topic for the quiz. I chose to make my quiz about dogs because I have one and they are my favorite animals. I went with a simple design and used some of my favorite colors for the design of the app.
